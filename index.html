Beyond Reaction: Proactive Flood Management

Traditional flood warnings are too slow for the rapid pace of urban life. We provide the foresight and tools to act proactively.

The Challenge

Sudden flash floods overwhelm city infrastructure with little to no warning, causing widespread disruption, economic loss, and endangering human lives. Reactive measures are often insufficient and costly.

Our Solution

A smart, integrated system that uses sensor data and an AI-powered prediction engine to provide localized, early warnings and suggest intelligent control strategies for mitigation.

An Integrated, Intelligent System

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology into five core modules.

Real-time Data Simulation

Continuously simulates rainfall and water level data to feed the prediction engine and test system resilience.

Random Forest AI Model

Predicts flash floods with high accuracy using a powerful Random Forest model trained on historical IMD weather data.

React-based Dashboard

A dynamic, component-based GIS dashboard showing risk gauges, forecast charts, and actionable alerts for city authorities.

Powered by Modern Technology

Our system is built on a robust, industry-standard technology stack to ensure reliability and performance.
